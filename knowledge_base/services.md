# Services

**Haircut** — A full consultation, wash, cut, and style. Typically 45 minutes.

**Trim** — A quick length maintenance cut with no major restyling. Typically 20 minutes.

**Balayage** — A hand-painted highlighting technique for natural, sun-kissed
dimension. Includes a gloss treatment. Typically 2.5–3 hours. Deposit required.

**Color Correction** — For fixing previous color work (including DIY box dye
or bleach mishaps). Requires an in-person consultation first to assess hair
condition; cannot be booked online without that consult. Typically 3+ hours.
Deposit required.
