# Deposit Policy

A deposit is required for the following services due to the length of
time reserved: color correction, balayage, and any appointment booked
for 90 minutes or longer. The deposit is $50, applied toward the final
service cost. Deposits are non-refundable for cancellations made within
24 hours of the appointment, per our cancellation policy.

Standard haircuts and trims under 90 minutes do not require a deposit.
